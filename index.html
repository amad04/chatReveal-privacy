ChatReveal

Support & Privacy Information

Overview

ChatReveal is an AI-powered app that analyzes conversations and helps you understand the signals behind messages. You can paste text or upload screenshots of chats to receive insights about interest, vibe, and suggested replies.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information.

  • Firebase Analytics (anonymous usage data)
  • Crashlytics (crash reporting)

Third-Party Services

This app uses The Movie Database (TMDB) API.

This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.

How We Use Data

  • Improve performance
  • Fix crashes
  • Understand usage behavior

Local Data

Preferences and analysis history are stored only on your device and are not shared externally.

Your Rights

You can delete the app at any time to remove all stored data.
